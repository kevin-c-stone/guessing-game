Biography

Kevin was born in Delray Beach, Florida and moved to a small town called Morganton in the foothills of North Carolina when he was 12 years old. From there he grew up and went to high school, after graduating high school, he went to Appalachian State University where he struggled and ended up moving to Michigan where he attended Oakland Community College graduating with an Associates Degree in Business Administration. He then transferred into Michigan State University where he graduated with a Bachelor's of Arts in Economics. He worked the whole time through college then ended up joining the enlisted ranks of the United States Navy as an Aviation Electronics Technician. He enjoys watching sports and playing sports, playing his guitar, and anything outdoors.
